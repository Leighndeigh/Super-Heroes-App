This page celebrates six of the most iconic Marvel Super Hereos who have stoof together to protect the planetfrom threats beyond imagination. Get to know the core members of the team:

Thor

The God of Thunder, hailing from Asguard, wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir. With immense strength, control over lightning, and warrior's honour, Thor fights to protect both Earth and the Nine Realms.

Iron Man (Tony Stark)

A billionaire genius, inventor, and philanthropist, Tony Stark suits up in cutting-edge powered armour as Iron Man. With tech-savvy brilliance and unwavering courage, he leads with brains and heart.

Hulk (Bruce Banner)

Mild-mannered scientist Bruce Banner transforms into the Unstoppable Hulk when angered. Fueled by raw strength and resilience, Hulk is a force of nature - and a hero with a heart.

Hawkeye (Clint Barton)

A master archer with unmatched accuaracy, Hawkeye brings sharp skills and quick instincts to the team. Though human, his bravery and loyalty make him a true Avenger.

Captain America

A symbol of hope and justice, Steve Rogers was transformed into a super-soldier during WWII. As Captain America , he wields his iconic shield and leads the Avengers with integrity and unwavering resolve.

Black Widow

A former spy and mysterious past, Natasha Romanoff uses her elite training, agility, and intelligence as Black Widow. She's a fearless fighter and a crucial strategist on the team.
